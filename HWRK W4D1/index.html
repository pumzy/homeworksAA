What is cereal?

A cereal is any grass cultivated for the edible components of its grain (botanically, a type of fruit called a caryopsis), composed of the endosperm, germ, and bran A Brief History of Cereal

What do I put inside my cereal?

  • Milk
  • Cereal
  • Sugar, maybe
  • Nutella, if you're feeling really brave

How do I go about making cereal

  1. Choose which cereal you would like to eat, and grab a bowl. We will be using this bowl to make our cereal
  2. Fill your bowl 3/4 of the way with the cereal. This is the optimal level.
  3. Fill the bowl with milk until it is 7/8ths full. It is impotant to ensure that you put in your milk only after the cereal is poured
  4. Depending on your mood, add some sugar or nutella
  5. Use a tablespoon to scoop individual mouthfuls of cereal and consume them
